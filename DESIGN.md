# Design Definition: HDKWA Danielson PD

This design system is a fusion of the **Apple "Museum Gallery" aesthetic** (spacious, typography-first, white surfaces) and the **Haidian Kaiwen Academy (HDKWA)** school identity. It is designed to be calm, authoritative, and instructional.

---

## 1. Core Visual Language

### Surfaces and Depth
- **Canvas:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`). The dominant surface for content clarity.
- **Section Tiles:** Parchment (`#F5F5F7`). Used for alternating sections or grouping related Danielson components.
- **Elevation:** Flat. No drop shadows. Depth is created through background color shifts and generous negative space.
- **Radius:** 12px for cards and buttons (Apple standard for a modern, soft feel).

### Action and Identity
- **Primary Blue (HDKWA Navy):** `#003366`. Used for primary navigation, headers, and essential structural elements.
- **Action Blue (Apple-Inspired):** `#0066CC`. Used for links and interactive utility buttons to signify "clickable."
- **HDKWA Gold:** `#C5A059`. Used exclusively for **Progress Tracking** and **Gamification** (badges, progress bars, "Completed" states).

---

## 2. Typography (Apple Standard)

- **Font Family:** `SF Pro Display`, `-apple-system`, `BlinkMacSystemFont`, `Segoe UI`, `Roboto`, `Helvetica`, `Arial`, sans-serif.
- **Headings:** SF Pro Display. Near-Black Ink (`#1D1D1F`). Aggressive negative letter-spacing (`-0.022em`) for headlines > 32px.
- **Body:** SF Pro Text. Set at **17px** for better readability. Color: `#1D1D1F`.
- **Labels:** Mono (e.g., `Geist Mono` or `monospace`) at 12px for "Source Tags" (e.g., `TLAC 3.0`) and Danielson component codes (e.g., `3c`).

---

## 3. Component Specifications

### The Actionable Module
- **Layout:** Two-column on desktop (Text Left, Video Right). Single column on mobile.
- **Video Embed:** 16:9 aspect ratio. 12px border radius.
- **Steps:** Numbered list using HDKWA Navy (`#003366`) for the digits.

### Progress Tracking
- **Checkboxes:** When checked, they fill with HDKWA Gold (`#C5A059`) with a white checkmark.
- **Domain Badges:** Circular icons with a Gold border and Navy icon, signifying mastery of a Danielson domain.

---

## 4. Logo Integration
- **Header:** The HDKWA logo (Navy/Gold) should be placed top-left, with at least 40px of breathing room on all sides.
- **Footer:** A simplified Gold crane icon used as a subtle "seal of quality."

---

## 5. Rationale
By using Apple's "Museum Gallery" layout, we ensure that the instructional videos (which are the core "product") are never crowded. The HDKWA Navy provides the academic "weight" and institutional trust, while the HDKWA Gold serves as the visual reward system for teacher professional growth.
