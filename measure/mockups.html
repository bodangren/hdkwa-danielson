Domain 3 / 3c: Cold Call (TLAC 3.0)

Engaging All Students: The Cold Call Technique

Source: TLAC 3.0 Alignment: 3c

"Call on students regardless of whether they have raised their hands to ensure every student is accountable for the learning."

Action Steps

  • 1

    Pose the question to the **entire class** first to maximize think time.

  • 2

    Pause for 3-5 seconds (Wait Time 1.0).

  • 3

    Call a student by name in a **warm, predictable** tone.

Watch Demo Video

Technique breakdown by @DangRenBo

Coach's Note

"Notice how the teacher keeps the tone conversational. It's not a 'gotcha,' it's an 'I want to hear your thinking' moment."
